2nd Annual Northwoods Pride Festival

June 23 from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.

Arbor Vitae, Wisconsin

Come to the best party of the year in Northern Wisconsin! Join us on June 23 from 10 a.m. until 6 p.m. at Fireman's Park in Arbor Vitae for a celebration of the lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, questioning, and ally community. Meet cool people, enjoy a potluck, bring a dish to pass, hang out at the beach, see a drag show, shop for arts and crafts, play family games, and more. There will be music and dancing! Read about more ways to get involved in here.
